Rangat - Middle Andaman Gateway

Rangat is a picturesque town located in Middle Andaman Island, serving as an important transit point and a destination rich in natural beauty and ecological significance. Known for its pristine beaches, dense mangrove forests, and turtle nesting sites, Rangat offers a unique blend of adventure and tranquility away from the more commercialized tourist spots.

Located approximately 170 kilometers north of Port Blair, Rangat is accessible by road and serves as the gateway to North Andaman. The town is surrounded by lush tropical forests and is famous for its eco-tourism opportunities, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Eco-Tourism Paradise

Rangat is renowned for its commitment to eco-tourism and conservation efforts. The area is home to several protected beaches where sea turtles come to nest, particularly the Olive Ridley turtles. The region also boasts extensive mangrove forests, pristine beaches like Amkunj Beach, and offers excellent opportunities for trekking, bird watching, and experiencing the untouched natural beauty of the Andaman Islands.

What to Expect

1

Amkunj Beach

Beach Experience

Pristine Coastal Beauty

Visit the beautiful Amkunj Beach, known for its clean white sand, clear blue waters, and peaceful environment. The beach is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying the natural surroundings without the crowds of more popular destinations.

2

Turtle Nesting Sites

Wildlife

Sea Turtle Conservation

Experience the magical sight of sea turtle nesting (seasonal). Rangat is one of the important nesting sites for Olive Ridley turtles. Guided tours are available during nesting season to observe this natural phenomenon while maintaining conservation protocols.

3

Yerrata Mangrove Creek

Eco-Tour

Mangrove Exploration

Explore the extensive mangrove forests through boat rides in Yerrata Creek. This eco-tour offers opportunities to spot various bird species, saltwater crocodiles, and understand the importance of mangrove ecosystems in coastal protection.

4

Forest Trekking & Nature Trails

Adventure

Tropical Forest Experience

Embark on guided trekking expeditions through the dense tropical forests surrounding Rangat. These trails offer opportunities to spot endemic flora and fauna, including various bird species, butterflies, and unique plant life.
