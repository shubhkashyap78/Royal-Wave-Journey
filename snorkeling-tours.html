Snorkeling Tours

Home Activities Snorkeling Tours

Discover Andaman's Underwater Paradise

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of Andaman and explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful marine life. Our professional snorkeling tours offer safe and exciting underwater adventures suitable for beginners and experienced snorkelers alike. Discover the hidden treasures beneath the waves with our certified guides and high-quality equipment.

Best Snorkeling Destinations

  • Elephant Beach - Pristine Coral Gardens
  • North Bay Island - Colorful Marine Life
  • Bharatpur Beach - Shallow Water Snorkeling
  • Jolly Buoy Island - Protected Marine Park
  • Red Skin Island - Untouched Coral Reefs
  • Cinque Island - Advanced Snorkeling
