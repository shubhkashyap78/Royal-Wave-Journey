Family Package 4 Nights / 5 Days - Port Blair, Havelock & Neil Island

Explore the best of Andaman with our 4 Nights / 5 Days family package covering Port Blair, Havelock Island, and Neil Island. This comprehensive package is designed to give every family member an unforgettable experience with a perfect blend of adventure, sightseeing, and relaxation.

Kids will love the water sports and beach activities while parents enjoy the scenic beauty and comfortable accommodations. Our experienced guides ensure safety and fun for the entire family throughout the journey.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Arrival & Port Blair Sightseeing

Morning

Airport Welcome & Hotel Check-in

Family welcome at airport with garlands, comfortable hotel check-in with family rooms, and welcome snacks for kids.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove & Samudrika Museum

Beach fun at Corbyn's Cove followed by a visit to Samudrika Marine Museum - fascinating for kids to learn about marine life.

Evening

Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Inspiring historical show at Cellular Jail, followed by dinner at a family restaurant in Port Blair.

2

Day 2 - Havelock Island

Morning

Ferry to Havelock

Scenic ferry ride to Havelock Island, resort check-in with family rooms, and welcome refreshments.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach

Spend the afternoon at the stunning Radhanagar Beach - swimming, beach games, and family photography session.

Evening

Sunset & Family Dinner

Breathtaking sunset at the beach followed by a family dinner with local Andaman cuisine.

3

Day 3 - Elephant Beach Water Sports

Morning

Elephant Beach Adventure

Family snorkeling, glass bottom boat ride, banana boat rides, and sea walking for older kids and adults.

Afternoon

Kalapathar Beach

Scenic Kalapathar Beach visit with unique black rock formations, family photography, and leisurely beach time.

Evening

Resort Pool & Relaxation

Kids pool time at resort, family games, and a relaxing evening with dinner at the resort.

4

Day 4 - Neil Island Day Trip

Morning

Neil Island Excursion

Ferry to Neil Island, visit Bharatpur Beach for glass bottom boat ride and coral viewing - kids will be amazed!

Afternoon

Natural Bridge & Laxmanpur Beach

Explore the famous Natural Bridge rock formation and enjoy the pristine Laxmanpur Beach with family.

Evening

Return to Havelock

Ferry back to Havelock, family dinner, and a fun evening with games and activities at the resort.

5

Day 5 - Departure

Morning

Breakfast & Check-out

Final family breakfast, check-out, souvenir shopping, and last beach walk before departure.

Afternoon

Ferry & Airport Transfer

Ferry to Port Blair, airport transfer, and departure with wonderful family memories and complimentary photo album.

Package Includes

  • 4 nights family accommodation
  • Daily breakfast for family
  • Airport pickup and drop
  • All ferry transfers
  • Elephant Beach water sports

  • Neil Island day trip
  • Family photography session

  • All sightseeing as per itinerary
