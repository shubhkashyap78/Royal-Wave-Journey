Jet Ski Ride in Andaman Islands

Feel the rush of speed as you ride a powerful jet ski across the crystal-clear waters of the Andaman Islands. Jet skiing is one of the most thrilling water sports activities available in Andaman, offering an adrenaline-pumping experience amidst stunning natural surroundings.

Whether you want to ride solo or with a partner, our jet ski rides are available at multiple locations across the Andaman Islands. Our certified instructors provide a quick briefing before you take control of the jet ski and zoom across the waves.

The best locations for jet skiing in Andaman include Corbyn's Cove Beach in Port Blair, North Bay Island, and Radhanagar Beach in Havelock Island. Each location offers calm, clear waters perfect for an exciting jet ski experience.

Jet Ski Highlights

Speed & Thrill

On Water

High Speed Adventure

Experience the thrill of riding a powerful jet ski at high speeds across the turquoise Andaman waters. Feel the wind in your hair and the spray of the sea as you navigate the waves.

Solo or Duo

Options

Ride Alone or with a Partner

Jet skis can accommodate 1-2 riders. Ride solo for full control or bring a partner for a shared adventure. Our instructors can also ride with beginners for a guided experience.

Safety

Safety

Safe & Supervised

All riders are provided with life jackets and a safety briefing before the ride. Our experienced operators supervise the entire session to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
