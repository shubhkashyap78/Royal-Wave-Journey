Tour Overview

Embark on the most comprehensive Andaman experience with our 5 nights 6 days complete island tour. This extensive journey covers Port Blair, Ross Island, Havelock, and Neil Island, offering you the perfect blend of history, adventure, natural beauty, and cultural experiences across the pristine Andaman archipelago.

Tour Highlights

  • Historic Cellular Jail and Light & Sound Show
  • Ross Island - British settlement ruins exploration
  • North Bay Island coral viewing and water sports
  • Radhanagar Beach - World's best beach experience
  • Elephant Beach snorkeling and adventure activities
  • Neil Island's pristine beaches and Natural Bridge
  • Kalapathar Beach sunset photography

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Port Blair

Your Andaman adventure begins with arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport where our representative will warmly welcome you. After smooth transfer to your hotel and check-in, visit the historic Cellular Jail National Memorial to understand India's freedom struggle. The day concludes with the spectacular Light and Sound Show at Cellular Jail, bringing history to life through dramatic narration and lighting effects. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 2

Ross Island & North Bay

After breakfast, embark on a fascinating boat ride to Ross Island, once the administrative headquarters of the British. Explore the ruins of British settlement including the Chief Commissioner's house, Government house, church, and cemetery, now beautifully overgrown with tropical vegetation. Continue to North Bay Island for coral viewing through glass bottom boats and enjoy various water sports activities. Experience the vibrant underwater world and colorful coral reefs before returning to Port Blair for overnight stay.

Day 3

Port Blair to Havelock

Check-out after breakfast and proceed to Phoenix Bay Jetty for your ferry journey to Havelock Island, the crown jewel of Andaman. Upon arrival at this tropical paradise, check-in at your beachfront resort and enjoy lunch with stunning ocean views. Spend the afternoon at the world-famous Radhanagar Beach (Beach No. 7), consistently rated as one of the world's best beaches. Experience pristine white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and witness one of the most spectacular sunsets. Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 4

Havelock Island Activities

Start with breakfast before embarking on an exciting boat excursion to Elephant Beach, renowned for its pristine coral reefs and diverse marine life. Enjoy professional snorkeling, scuba diving (optional), sea walking, and various water sports in this underwater paradise. After lunch at the beach, visit Kalapathar Beach known for its unique black rocks contrasting with white sand and blue waters. Spend the evening at leisure at your resort, enjoying the tropical ambiance and beachfront facilities. Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 5

Havelock to Neil Island

After breakfast, take the ferry to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep), known for its laid-back atmosphere and pristine beaches. Check-in at your Neil Island accommodation and visit Bharatpur Beach, famous for its coral formations and clear waters perfect for swimming. Explore Laxmanpur Beach with its stunning rock formations and peaceful ambiance. Visit the Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge), a fascinating coral formation created by nature over thousands of years. Enjoy the serene beauty and tranquility of this less crowded island. Overnight stay at Neil Island.

Day 6

Departure

Experience the magical sunrise at Sitapur Beach, one of the most beautiful sunrise points in Andaman. After breakfast and check-out, take the ferry back to Port Blair. Spend time shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar and Sagarika Government Emporium for souvenirs, local handicrafts, pearls, shell items, and wooden artifacts. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before transferring to the airport for your departure, carrying with you wonderful memories of your comprehensive Andaman island adventure.
