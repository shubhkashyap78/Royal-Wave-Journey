Extended Romantic Honeymoon Package 4 Nights / 5 Days

Indulge in a more comprehensive romantic experience with our 4 nights 5 days honeymoon package in the enchanting Andaman Islands. This extended package offers additional time to explore more destinations, enjoy enhanced romantic experiences, and create deeper connections with your loved one amidst paradise.

This package includes visits to Port Blair, Havelock Island, Neil Island, and Elephant Beach, offering the perfect combination of adventure, relaxation, and romance. Experience couple spa sessions, professional photography, welcome drinks, and many more romantic surprises designed exclusively for honeymooners.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Arrival & Port Blair Exploration

Morning

Grand Welcome & Hotel Check-in

VIP airport pickup with flower garlands and welcome drinks, luxury hotel check-in with honeymoon suite decoration, and personalized welcome amenities.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove Beach & Water Sports

Romantic beach visit with couple water sports activities, jet skiing, and banana boat rides followed by beachside refreshments.

Evening

Cellular Jail & Romantic Dinner

Historical light and sound show at Cellular Jail, followed by a special romantic dinner at a premium seaside restaurant with live music.

2

Day 2 - Havelock Island Paradise

Morning

Ferry to Havelock & Resort Check-in

Luxury ferry transfer to Havelock Island, check-in at premium beach resort with ocean view room, and welcome refreshments with tropical fruits.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach Experience

Visit Asia's best beach with professional couple photography session, romantic beach walk, and private beach picnic setup.

Evening

Sunset & Candlelight Dinner

Romantic sunset viewing followed by exclusive candlelight dinner on the beach with personalized menu and live acoustic music.

3

Day 3 - Adventure & Relaxation

Morning

Elephant Beach Water Adventures

Couple snorkeling experience at Elephant Beach, explore vibrant coral reefs together, sea walking, and underwater photography.

Afternoon

Couple Spa Session

Relaxing couple spa treatment at the resort with traditional Ayurvedic massage, aromatherapy, and rejuvenation therapies.

Evening

Kalapathar Beach Photography

Professional photography session at scenic Kalapathar Beach with unique black rocks backdrop and golden hour lighting.

4

Day 4 - Neil Island Exploration

Morning

Neil Island Day Trip

Ferry to Neil Island, visit Bharatpur Beach for glass bottom boat ride and coral viewing, romantic beach activities.

Afternoon

Natural Bridge & Laxmanpur Beach

Explore Natural Bridge rock formation, visit Laxmanpur Beach for sunset views, and enjoy couple time in pristine surroundings.

Evening

Return to Havelock

Return ferry to Havelock, romantic dinner at resort with special honeymoon cake and surprise arrangements.

5

Day 5 - Farewell & Departure

Morning

Leisure Time & Check-out

Final romantic breakfast, leisure time at resort, couple's last beach walk, check-out with complimentary photo album and souvenirs.

Afternoon

Return Journey

Ferry transfer to Port Blair, airport transfer for departure with beautiful memories and personalized honeymoon certificate.
