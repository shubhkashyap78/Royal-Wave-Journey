Makruzz – Premium High-Speed Ferry

Makruzz is the Andaman Islands' most popular and premium high-speed catamaran ferry service, connecting Port Blair to Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep) and Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) in comfort and style. Known for its speed, reliability, and world-class onboard experience, Makruzz is the preferred choice for travellers seeking a smooth and enjoyable inter-island journey.

With a capacity of over 250 passengers, air-conditioned interiors, comfortable reclining seats, and a dedicated crew, Makruzz ensures your journey across the Andaman Sea is as memorable as the destination itself.

Ferry Highlights

High-Speed Catamaran

Speed

Fast & Reliable Travel

Makruzz covers the Port Blair to Havelock route in approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, making it the fastest ferry option available in the Andamans. Modern catamaran design ensures minimal sea sickness even in rough waters.

Comfortable Interiors

Onboard

Premium Seating & Amenities

Fully air-conditioned cabin with wide reclining seats, large panoramic windows, clean restrooms, and a snack counter onboard. Entertainment screens and life jackets provided for all passengers.

Routes Covered

Routes

Port Blair ↔ Havelock ↔ Neil Island

Makruzz operates daily scheduled services between Port Blair (Phoenix Bay Jetty), Havelock Island, and Neil Island. Multiple departure slots available in the morning and afternoon.

Booking & Tickets

Booking

Advance Booking Recommended

Tickets sell out fast, especially during peak season (October to May). We handle all ferry bookings on your behalf as part of your tour package, ensuring confirmed seats without any hassle.
