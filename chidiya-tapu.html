Chidiya Tapu - Bird Island

Chidiya Tapu, also known as Bird Island, is located about 25 kilometers south of Port Blair in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. This picturesque destination is renowned for its spectacular sunset views and diverse bird species, making it a paradise for nature lovers and bird watchers.

The name "Chidiya Tapu" literally translates to "Bird Island" in Hindi, reflecting the rich avian diversity found in this region. The area is surrounded by dense mangrove forests and pristine beaches, creating a perfect habitat for various species of birds.

Sunset Point Paradise

Chidiya Tapu is famous for offering one of the most breathtaking sunset views in the Andaman Islands. The golden hour here creates a magical atmosphere as the sun sets over the Bay of Bengal, painting the sky in vibrant hues of orange, pink, and purple. This natural spectacle attracts photographers, couples, and nature enthusiasts from around the world.
