Grand Romantic Honeymoon Package 8 Nights / 9 Days

Immerse yourselves in the ultimate Andaman honeymoon with our 8 nights 9 days grand romantic package. Crafted for couples who want to see it all, this journey blends luxury stays, unforgettable island experiences, and a curated mix of romance, adventure, and relaxation.

Covering Port Blair, Havelock, Neil Island, Ross Island, North Bay, and Baratang, this itinerary delivers iconic beaches, private experiences, multiple photo sessions, couple spa therapies, and romantic dinners that celebrate your love in paradise.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Grand Arrival & Port Blair Welcome

Morning

VIP Airport Reception & Luxury Check-in

Warm floral welcome, premium transfers, and honeymoon suite check-in with romantic decor, welcome drinks, and couple amenities.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove Beach Leisure

Relax at Corbyn's Cove with beachside refreshments, couple time by the waves, and optional water sports.

Evening

Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Historic light and sound show followed by a romantic dinner at a seaside restaurant.

2

Day 2 - Journey to Havelock Island

Morning

Ferry to Havelock & Resort Check-in

Premium ferry transfer to Havelock Island, beach resort check-in, and tropical welcome refreshments.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach Romance

Visit Asia's best beach, enjoy a private beach setup, and a romantic sunset stroll.

Evening

Candlelight Beach Dinner

Exclusive candlelight dinner on the beach with personalized menu and soft music.

3

Day 3 - Havelock Adventure & Wellness

Morning

Elephant Beach Snorkeling

Couple snorkeling at Elephant Beach with coral views, sea walking options, and underwater photography.

Afternoon

Couple Spa Session

Relaxing couple spa therapy with aromatherapy and rejuvenating massages at the resort.

Evening

Kalapathar Beach Photoshoot

Golden hour photography at Kalapathar Beach followed by a romantic dinner at the resort.

4

Day 4 - Leisure & Private Moments

Morning

Lazy Breakfast & Beach Time

Slow morning with in-room breakfast, private beach time, and optional kayaking or paddle boarding.

Afternoon

Professional Couple Photography

Second professional photography session capturing curated couple portraits around the resort and shoreline.

Evening

Private Candlelit Setup

Exclusive private setup with mood lighting, dessert platter, and a honeymoon surprise.

5

Day 5 - Neil Island Discovery

Morning

Ferry to Neil Island & Natural Bridge

Scenic ferry to Neil Island, visit the Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge) and explore coral formations.

Afternoon

Bharatpur Beach & Glass Bottom Boat

Glass bottom boat ride, coral viewing, and beach leisure with tropical refreshments.

Evening

Laxmanpur Sunset & Island Dinner

Romantic sunset at Laxmanpur Beach followed by a candlelight dinner on Neil Island.

6

Day 6 - Ross Island & North Bay

Morning

Return to Port Blair & Ross Island Tour

Check-out from Neil Island, ferry back to Port Blair, explore historic Ross Island ruins and scenic viewpoints.

Afternoon

North Bay Water Sports

Snorkeling, glass bottom boat rides, and water sports at North Bay with vibrant reef views.

Evening

Seaside Dinner & Leisure

Relaxing evening with a seaside dinner and couple time at the hotel.

7

Day 7 - Baratang Day Excursion

Morning

Mangrove Creek & Limestone Caves

Early drive to Baratang, boat ride through mangrove creeks, and guided visit to limestone caves.

Afternoon

Tribal Reserve Viewpoint & Picnic

Scenic viewpoints en route with a packed picnic lunch and return to Port Blair.

Evening

Relaxed Evening & Spa

Optional spa session and leisure time, followed by a romantic dinner.

8

Day 8 - Chidiya Tapu & Farewell Celebrations

Morning

Leisure Morning & Shopping

Relaxed breakfast, souvenir shopping at local markets, and free time for couple activities.

Afternoon

Chidiya Tapu Beach Escape

Visit Chidiya Tapu for a serene beach escape, nature trail, and romantic photo moments.

Evening

Farewell Dinner & Honeymoon Surprises

Grand farewell dinner with honeymoon cake, special decor, and a curated surprise gift.

9

Day 9 - Departure with Memories

Morning

Check-out & Airport Transfer

Leisurely breakfast, check-out, and airport transfer with complimentary photo album and honeymoon certificate.

Afternoon

Warm Farewell

Departure with lifelong memories of the Andaman Islands and your romantic journey.
