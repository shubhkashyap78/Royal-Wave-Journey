Port Blair, Havelock and Cellular Jail Tour Overview

Step into a world that resembles a perfect dream - exotic, stunning, and deeply connected to India's rich colonial heritage. The Andaman and Nicobar Islands have emerged as one of the most sought-after travel destinations in recent years.

Whether you seek a peaceful beach retreat or an action-packed adventure filled with trekking, snorkeling, and scuba diving experiences, Andaman tourism offers the perfect escape from busy city life while providing all modern conveniences. This is what makes the Andaman Islands truly special.

Port Blair, Havelock and Cellular Jail Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival at Port Blair

Arrival at Port Blair in the morning and transfer to respective hotel. Later on, proceed to Cellular Jail visit and Carbyn beach. Late evening witness the enthralling Sound and Light Show at Cellular Jail - where the heroic saga of the Indian freedom struggle is brought alive. Overnight stay at respective Hotel / Resort at Port Blair.

Day 2

Port Blair to Havelock / Overnight stay at Havelock Island

After breakfast, proceed to the jetty for your ferry journey to Havelock Island. Check into your resort upon arrival and spend the day at leisure exploring the beautiful surroundings.

Overnight: Radha Krishna Resort, Havelock Island

Day 3

Excursion to Elephant beach with 01 session of Snorkeling / Havelock Island to Port Blair

Visit the famous Elephant Beach and enjoy one complimentary snorkeling session to explore the underwater marine life and coral formations.

Later, return to Port Blair by ferry and check into your hotel.

Overnight: Coast Land Hotel, Port Blair

Day 4

Departure

Enjoy breakfast and complete check-out formalities. Transfer to airport for your onward journey, carrying unforgettable memories of the Andaman Islands.
