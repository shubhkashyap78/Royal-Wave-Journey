Banana Ride in Andaman Islands

The Banana Ride is one of the most popular and fun water sports activities in the Andaman Islands. Hop on the inflatable banana-shaped boat and hold on tight as a speedboat pulls you across the sparkling blue waters at thrilling speeds. It is a perfect activity for families, friends, and groups looking for some fun and laughter on the water.

Available at North Bay Island, Corbyn's Cove Beach, and Elephant Beach, the banana ride can accommodate multiple riders at once, making it a great group activity. Life jackets are provided for all riders and safety is our top priority.

Whether you stay on or get tossed into the warm Andaman waters, the banana ride guarantees non-stop fun and excitement for everyone. It is suitable for all age groups and requires no prior experience.

Banana Ride Highlights

Fun for Everyone

All Ages

Family & Group Activity

The banana ride can accommodate 4-8 riders at a time, making it perfect for families and groups. Kids and adults alike love the thrill of riding the inflatable banana boat across the waves.

Safety First

Safety

Fully Equipped & Safe

All riders are provided with life jackets before the ride. Our experienced operators ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. The ride takes place in calm, shallow waters close to the shore.

Best Locations

Locations

Top Spots for Banana Ride

North Bay Island, Corbyn's Cove Beach in Port Blair, and Elephant Beach in Havelock Island are the best spots for banana rides in Andaman, offering calm waters and stunning surroundings.
