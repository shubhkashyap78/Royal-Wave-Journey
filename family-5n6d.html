Family Package 5 Nights / 6 Days - Port Blair, Havelock, Neil & Ross Island

Our 5 Nights / 6 Days family package offers an extended and enriching Andaman experience covering Port Blair, Havelock Island, Neil Island, and the historic Ross Island. This package is perfect for families who want to explore the islands thoroughly with ample time at each destination.

From heritage tours at Ross Island to snorkeling adventures at Elephant Beach, every day brings new excitement for the whole family. Our kid-friendly itinerary ensures children are engaged and entertained throughout the trip.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Arrival & Port Blair

Morning

Airport Welcome & Hotel Check-in

Family welcome at airport, comfortable hotel check-in with family rooms, and welcome refreshments.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove Beach & Samudrika Museum

Beach fun at Corbyn's Cove, visit Samudrika Marine Museum to learn about Andaman's marine biodiversity.

Evening

Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Educational light & sound show at Cellular Jail followed by dinner at a family restaurant.

2

Day 2 - Ross Island & North Bay

Morning

Ross Island Heritage Tour

Boat trip to Ross Island, explore British-era ruins, deer spotting, and peacock watching - kids love the wildlife!

Afternoon

North Bay Island Water Sports

Snorkeling, glass bottom boat ride, and sea walking at North Bay Island - an underwater adventure for the family.

Evening

Port Blair Local Market

Evening stroll at Aberdeen Bazaar, souvenir shopping, and dinner at a local restaurant.

3

Day 3 - Havelock Island

Morning

Ferry to Havelock

Scenic ferry ride to Havelock Island, resort check-in with family rooms, and welcome refreshments.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach

Spend the afternoon at Asia's best beach - swimming, beach games, and family photography session.

Evening

Sunset & Family Dinner

Stunning sunset views followed by a delicious family dinner at the resort.

4

Day 4 - Elephant Beach & Kalapathar

Morning

Elephant Beach Water Adventures

Family snorkeling, banana boat rides, jet skiing for adults, and safe water activities for kids at Elephant Beach.

Afternoon

Kalapathar Beach Photography

Family photography session at scenic Kalapathar Beach with unique black rock formations and turquoise waters.

Evening

Resort Leisure & Dinner

Relaxing evening at resort with kids activities, pool time, and family dinner.

5

Day 5 - Neil Island

Morning

Neil Island Day Trip

Ferry to Neil Island, visit Bharatpur Beach for glass bottom boat ride and coral viewing.

Afternoon

Natural Bridge & Laxmanpur Beach

Explore the Natural Bridge rock formation and enjoy Laxmanpur Beach with the family.

Evening

Return & Farewell Dinner

Return to Havelock, special farewell family dinner with cake and surprise arrangements.

6

Day 6 - Departure

Morning

Breakfast & Check-out

Final family breakfast, check-out, and last souvenir shopping for the kids.

Afternoon

Ferry & Airport Transfer

Ferry to Port Blair, airport transfer, and departure with beautiful family memories and complimentary photo album.
