Green Ocean – Comfortable Inter-Island Ferry

Green Ocean is a well-established ferry service operating in the Andaman Islands, offering comfortable and affordable inter-island travel between Port Blair, Havelock Island, and Neil Island. Known for its spacious decks and reliable schedule, Green Ocean is a popular choice among budget-conscious travellers and families.

With both upper and lower deck seating options, Green Ocean provides a relaxed journey across the Andaman Sea, allowing passengers to enjoy the open sea breeze and stunning ocean views during the crossing.

Ferry Highlights

Spacious Ferry Design

Comfort

Upper & Lower Deck Options

Green Ocean features both enclosed lower deck seating with fans and an open upper deck for passengers who enjoy the sea breeze and panoramic views. Ample space for families and groups travelling together.

Schedule & Timings

Daily

Regular Daily Departures

Green Ocean operates daily scheduled services with morning departures from Phoenix Bay Jetty, Port Blair. The journey to Havelock takes approximately 2 to 2.5 hours, offering a leisurely and scenic crossing.

Routes Covered

Routes

Port Blair ↔ Havelock ↔ Neil Island

Covers the main inter-island routes connecting Port Blair, Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep), and Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep). Ideal for day trips and multi-island itineraries.

Affordable Travel

Value

Budget-Friendly Option

Green Ocean offers one of the most affordable ferry options in the Andamans without compromising on safety or comfort. A great choice for families, groups, and budget travellers looking to explore multiple islands.
