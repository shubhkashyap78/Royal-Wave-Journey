Nautika – Modern High-Speed Ferry

Nautika is one of the newer and most modern high-speed ferry services operating in the Andaman Islands. With its sleek design, powerful engines, and premium onboard amenities, Nautika offers a fast and comfortable inter-island travel experience between Port Blair, Havelock Island, and Neil Island.

Nautika is designed for travellers who value speed, comfort, and a premium experience. The vessel features plush seating, large windows with ocean views, and a smooth ride even in choppy sea conditions, making it a top choice for discerning travellers.

Ferry Highlights

Premium Experience

Comfort

Plush Seating & Modern Interiors

Nautika features premium reclining seats, wide panoramic windows, fully air-conditioned interiors, and a modern aesthetic that sets it apart from other ferry services in the Andamans.

High Speed

Speed

Fast Inter-Island Crossings

Nautika's powerful engines ensure fast crossings between islands, covering the Port Blair to Havelock route in approximately 1.5 to 2 hours. Smooth hull design minimises wave impact for a comfortable ride.

Routes Covered

Routes

Port Blair ↔ Havelock ↔ Neil Island

Nautika operates daily scheduled services on the main inter-island routes. Multiple departure slots available, making it easy to fit into any tour itinerary.

Easy Booking

Booking

Hassle-Free Ticket Booking

We handle all Nautika ferry bookings as part of your tour package. Advance booking is strongly recommended during peak season (October to May) as seats fill up quickly.
