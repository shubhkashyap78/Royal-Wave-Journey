Tour Overview

Experience the ultimate luxury getaway with our premium 3 nights 4 days Port Blair - Havelock tour. This carefully curated package offers an enhanced travel experience with premium accommodations, exclusive services, and personalized attention, making it perfect for honeymooners, couples, and discerning travelers seeking comfort and elegance.

Tour Highlights

  • VIP meet and greet service at airport
  • Premium beachfront resort accommodation
  • Anthropological Museum visit
  • Marina Park evening stroll
  • Radhanagar Beach exclusive experience
  • Complimentary spa session
  • Candlelight dinner on beach

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Welcome to Andaman

Your premium Andaman experience begins with VIP arrival service at Veer Savarkar International Airport. Our dedicated representative will provide personalized meet and greet service, followed by luxury transfer to your premium hotel. After check-in with welcome drinks, visit the fascinating Anthropological Museum to learn about indigenous tribes. Spend a peaceful evening at Marina Park, enjoying the waterfront ambiance and local culture. Return to hotel for overnight stay with premium amenities.

Day 2

Journey to Paradise - Havelock

Start with a luxurious breakfast before boarding the premium ferry to Havelock Island. Upon arrival at this tropical paradise, check-in at your exclusive beachfront resort with ocean views. Enjoy a welcome lunch featuring fresh seafood and local delicacies. Spend the afternoon at the world-famous Radhanagar Beach with private beach setup, complete with comfortable seating and refreshments. Experience a professional sunset photography session followed by a romantic beachside dinner under the stars.

Day 3

Adventure & Relaxation

Begin with optional sunrise yoga session on the beach, followed by breakfast at your resort. Embark on an exclusive Elephant Beach adventure trip with private boat and professional guide. Enjoy premium snorkeling experience with high-quality equipment and underwater photography. Savor a gourmet beach picnic lunch with tropical fruits and beverages. Return to resort for a complimentary spa session featuring traditional Ayurvedic treatments. End the day with a special candlelight dinner on the beach, complete with live acoustic music.

Day 4

Farewell to Paradise

Enjoy your final breakfast with panoramic ocean views before check-out with late departure facility. Take the premium ferry back to Port Blair with comfortable seating and onboard refreshments. Visit the historic Cellular Jail National Memorial with expert guide providing detailed historical insights. Enjoy shopping at the exclusive Sagarika Emporium for authentic souvenirs, pearls, and handicrafts. Conclude with lunch at a premium restaurant before luxury transfer to airport for departure, carrying unforgettable memories of your premium Andaman experience.
