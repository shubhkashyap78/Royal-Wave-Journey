ITT Majestic – Reliable Inter-Island Ferry

ITT Majestic is a trusted ferry service operating in the Andaman Islands, providing reliable and comfortable inter-island transportation between Port Blair, Havelock Island, and Neil Island. Operated by ITT (Island Travel & Tourism), the Majestic ferry is known for its punctuality, safety standards, and comfortable onboard experience.

Whether you are travelling for leisure or as part of a tour package, ITT Majestic offers a dependable and pleasant sea journey across the Andaman waters, making it a preferred choice for both tourists and locals alike.

Ferry Highlights

Safety & Reliability

Safety

High Safety Standards

ITT Majestic adheres to strict maritime safety regulations with regular vessel maintenance, trained crew, and all necessary safety equipment including life jackets, life rafts, and emergency communication systems.

Comfortable Seating

Onboard

AC & Non-AC Seating Options

ITT Majestic offers both air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned seating options to suit different budgets and preferences. The vessel has a spacious interior with comfortable seating arrangements for all passengers.

Routes Covered

Routes

Port Blair ↔ Havelock ↔ Neil Island

ITT Majestic operates on the key inter-island routes connecting Port Blair (Phoenix Bay Jetty), Havelock Island, and Neil Island with daily scheduled departures. Ideal for multi-island tour itineraries.

Punctual Service

Schedule

On-Time Departures

ITT Majestic is known for its punctual departures and arrivals, making it a reliable choice for travellers with tight itineraries. We coordinate all ferry bookings to ensure seamless connections between islands.
