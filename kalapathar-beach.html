KalaPathar Beach - Hidden Gem

Kalapathar Beach is one of the many hidden gems of Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep) in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Known for its serene ambiance, distinctive black rocks, and picturesque surroundings, Kalapathar Beach offers a tranquil retreat away from the more crowded tourist spots.

Kalapathar Beach is located on the southeastern coast of Havelock Island. The beach is easily accessible by road, about a 15-20 minute drive from the Havelock Island jetty.

Tranquil Retreat

Kalapathar Beach on Havelock Island is a serene and picturesque destination, perfect for those looking to escape the crowds and enjoy a peaceful day by the sea. Its unique landscape, with striking black rocks and clear blue waters, offers a memorable experience for visitors. Whether you're taking a leisurely walk, enjoying a picnic, or simply relaxing under the shade of trees, Kalapathar Beach provides a tranquil retreat amidst the natural beauty of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

Key Features

1

Distinctive Black Rocks

Unique Feature

Photogenic Landscape

The beach is named after the distinctive black rocks (kalapathar) that are scattered along the shoreline, offering a unique and photogenic landscape.

2

Soft White Sandy Beach

Beach Quality

Perfect for Relaxation

The soft, white sandy stretch of the beach is perfect for leisurely walks and relaxation.

3

Crystal Clear Waters

Water Quality

Scenic Beauty

The crystal-clear waters of the Andaman Sea add to the beach's charm, making it a great spot for wading and enjoying the view.

4

Tranquil Environment

Peaceful

Serene Atmosphere

Kalapathar Beach is primarily known for its tranquil and serene environment, making it ideal for those looking to relax and unwind. Visitors often enjoy long, peaceful walks along the shoreline, taking in the natural beauty and calm atmosphere.

5

Basic Amenities

Facilities

Simple Comfort

While the beach is relatively less developed compared to other tourist spots on Havelock Island, there are a few basic amenities such as benches and shaded areas.
