Ultimate Romantic Honeymoon Package 6 Nights / 7 Days

Experience the most comprehensive and luxurious romantic journey with our 6 nights 7 days ultimate honeymoon package in the breathtaking Andaman Islands. This premium package offers the perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, romance, and luxury experiences designed exclusively for couples seeking an unforgettable honeymoon.

This extensive package covers Port Blair, Havelock Island, Neil Island, Ross Island, and North Bay, providing complete island exploration with enhanced romantic experiences, multiple couple spa sessions, professional photography, VIP treatments, and exclusive honeymoon surprises that will create memories to last a lifetime.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Grand Arrival & Port Blair Welcome

Morning

VIP Airport Reception & Luxury Check-in

Premium airport pickup with flower garlands and welcome drinks, luxury hotel check-in with honeymoon suite upgrade, room decoration with rose petals, candles, and champagne.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove Beach & Romantic Lunch

Visit to scenic Corbyn's Cove Beach with couple activities, romantic beachside lunch, and leisure time with tropical fruit platter and coconut water.

Evening

Sunset Dinner Cruise

Exclusive romantic sunset dinner cruise with live entertainment, premium dining, and breathtaking ocean views as you sail around Port Blair.

2

Day 2 - Port Blair Heritage & Romance

Morning

Cellular Jail & Historical Tour

Visit historic Cellular Jail National Memorial, explore Chatham Saw Mill, Forest Museum, and Anthropological Museum with guided heritage tour.

Afternoon

Professional Photography & Shopping

First professional couple photoshoot at scenic Port Blair locations, shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar for pearls and local souvenirs.

Evening

Light & Sound Show + Candlelight Dinner

Mesmerizing Light and Sound Show at Cellular Jail followed by romantic candlelight dinner at hotel's premium rooftop restaurant.

3

Day 3 - Journey to Paradise - Havelock Island

Morning

Premium Ferry to Havelock & Resort Check-in

VIP transfer to Phoenix Bay Jetty, luxury ferry journey to Havelock Island, check-in at premium beach resort with ocean view suite and welcome amenities.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach Experience

Visit Asia's best beach - Radhanagar Beach, romantic beach walk, couple activities, and relaxation time with beachside refreshments.

Evening

Sunset Photography & Beach Dinner

Professional sunset photography session at Radhanagar Beach followed by private beach dinner with bonfire setup and live acoustic music.

4

Day 4 - Havelock Adventure & Wellness

Morning

Elephant Beach Water Adventures

Exciting trip to Elephant Beach with couple snorkeling, sea walking experience, jet skiing, banana boat rides, and underwater coral exploration.

Afternoon

Couple Spa & Wellness Session

Rejuvenating couple spa treatment at resort with traditional Ayurvedic massage, aromatherapy, and complete wellness experience for couples.

Evening

Kalapathar Beach & Romantic Dinner

Visit scenic Kalapathar Beach with unique black rocks, second professional photoshoot, followed by romantic dinner under the stars.

5

Day 5 - Neil Island Discovery

Morning

Neil Island Ferry & Natural Bridge

Ferry journey to Neil Island, visit to Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge) rock formation, and exploration of this pristine coral island.

Afternoon

Bharatpur Beach & Glass Bottom Boat

Visit Bharatpur Beach for glass bottom boat ride, coral viewing, marine life observation, and romantic beach activities with lunch.

Evening

Laxmanpur Beach Sunset & Return

Visit Laxmanpur Beach for spectacular sunset views, beach cycling, return ferry to Havelock, and special dinner at resort.

6

Day 6 - Ross Island & North Bay Adventure

Morning

Return to Port Blair & Ross Island

Check-out from Havelock, ferry back to Port Blair, visit historic Ross Island with British-era ruins, peacock sightings, and guided heritage walk.

Afternoon

North Bay Island Water Sports

Exciting trip to North Bay Island for scuba diving, helmet diving, glass bottom boat ride, and various water sports activities for couples.

Evening

Farewell Spa & Cultural Dinner

Final couple massage and spa treatments, farewell dinner with cultural dance performance, and special honeymoon cake celebration.

7

Day 7 - Memorable Departure

Morning

Leisure Time & Final Shopping

Leisurely breakfast, visit to Samudrika Naval Marine Museum, last-minute shopping at Sagarika Emporium for pearl jewelry and handicrafts.

Afternoon

VIP Departure

Hotel check-out, VIP airport transfer, departure assistance, complimentary photo album, souvenir hamper, and honeymoon certificate presentation.
