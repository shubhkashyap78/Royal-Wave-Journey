Tour Overview

Embark on the ultimate luxury Andaman experience with our comprehensive 5 nights 6 days tour package. This premium journey covers Port Blair, South Andaman, Havelock, and Neil Island, offering the perfect blend of luxury accommodation, exclusive experiences, adventure activities, and cultural immersion in the pristine Andaman archipelago.

Tour Highlights

  • VIP arrival with luxury transfers
  • 5-star hotel accommodation
  • South Andaman full day exploration
  • Chidiya Tapu sunset point visit
  • Luxury ferry to Havelock Island
  • Professional scuba diving session
  • Spa and wellness treatments
  • Romantic dinner under stars

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Grand Arrival in Andaman

Experience VIP arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport with luxury transfer to your 5-star hotel. Enjoy welcome drinks and premium check-in service. Attend an orientation session about Andaman's history and culture. Visit the informative Fisheries Museum to learn about marine biodiversity. Spend the evening at Aberdeen Clock Tower area, exploring local markets and culture. Conclude with a welcome dinner at your hotel featuring local and international cuisine.

Day 2

South Andaman Exploration

After breakfast, embark on a full day South Andaman tour. Visit Chidiya Tapu (Bird Island), famous for its diverse bird species and stunning sunset views. Explore Munda Pahad Beach with its unique rock formations and pristine shoreline. Enjoy a scenic lunch at a picturesque location overlooking the ocean. Experience the breathtaking sunset at Chidiya Tapu sunset point, one of the most spectacular sunset viewing locations in Andaman. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 3

Journey to Havelock Paradise

Board the luxury ferry to Havelock Island, enjoying comfortable seating and onboard refreshments. Check-in at your premium beach resort with ocean views and welcome lunch featuring fresh seafood. Spend the afternoon at the world-famous Radhanagar Beach, consistently rated as one of the world's best beaches. Enjoy beach activities, water sports, and professional photography. Experience sunset cocktails on the beach followed by a special beachside barbecue dinner with live entertainment.

Day 4

Havelock Adventure Day

Begin with sunrise yoga session on the beach, followed by breakfast at your resort. Embark on an Elephant Beach adventure excursion with professional guides. Experience scuba diving with certified instructors, exploring vibrant coral reefs and marine life. Enjoy underwater photography and snorkeling in crystal-clear waters. Savor a beach picnic lunch with tropical fruits and beverages. Return to resort for spa and wellness session featuring traditional treatments. End with a romantic dinner under the stars with acoustic music.

Day 5

Neil Island Discovery

Take the ferry to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) and check-in at an eco-friendly resort. Visit Bharatpur Beach for coral viewing and swimming in clear waters. Explore Laxmanpur Beach with its stunning rock formations and peaceful ambiance. Visit the Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge), a fascinating geological formation. Enjoy a glass bottom boat ride to observe colorful coral reefs and tropical fish. In the evening, return to Port Blair by ferry and check-in at your hotel for overnight stay.

Day 6

Heritage & Farewell

Visit the historic Cellular Jail National Memorial with an expert guide providing detailed insights into India's freedom struggle. Experience the emotional Light and Sound Show that brings history to life. Enjoy shopping at Sagarika Government Emporium for authentic souvenirs, pearls, handicrafts, and wooden artifacts. Conclude with a farewell lunch at a premium restaurant featuring local delicacies. Transfer to airport with luxury service for departure, carrying lifetime memories of your comprehensive Andaman experience.
