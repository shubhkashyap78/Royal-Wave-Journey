Parasailing in Andaman Islands

Experience the thrill of soaring high above the crystal-clear waters of the Andaman Islands with our exciting parasailing adventure. Parasailing in Andaman offers breathtaking aerial views of the turquoise sea, white sandy beaches, and lush green islands that you simply cannot get from the ground.

Whether you are a first-timer or an experienced thrill-seeker, parasailing in Andaman is an experience that will leave you with unforgettable memories. Our certified operators ensure complete safety while giving you the most exhilarating ride of your life.

The best locations for parasailing in Andaman include North Bay Island and Corbyn's Cove Beach in Port Blair, and Radhanagar Beach in Havelock Island. Each spot offers stunning views and calm sea conditions perfect for a safe and enjoyable parasailing experience.

Parasailing Highlights

Safety Briefing

Before Ride

Pre-Flight Instructions

Our certified instructors provide a complete safety briefing before the parasailing session. You will be equipped with a life jacket and harness and guided through the entire process step by step.

The Aerial Experience

In Air

Soar Above the Sea

Rise up to 100-150 feet above the sea and enjoy panoramic views of the Andaman Islands. Feel the gentle sea breeze as you glide through the air with the stunning blue ocean below you.

Memories

Capture the Moment

Our team captures photos and videos of your parasailing experience from the boat. Take home stunning aerial shots of the Andaman Islands as a souvenir of your adventure.
