Group Package 4 Nights / 5 Days - Port Blair, Havelock & Neil Island

Kick off your group adventure with our 4 Nights / 5 Days Andaman group package covering Port Blair, Havelock Island, and Neil Island. Ideal for corporate groups, college trips, friends outings, and any group of 10 or more looking for an exciting and well-organized island getaway.

With a dedicated group guide, comfortable group transport, and specially curated group activities, this package ensures every member of your group has an amazing time exploring the beautiful Andaman Islands.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Arrival & Port Blair

Morning

Group Welcome & Hotel Check-in

Group welcome at airport, AC coach transfer to hotel, group check-in with comfortable rooms, and welcome refreshments.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove Beach & Water Sports

Group beach activities at Corbyn's Cove, water sports, beach games, and group photography session.

Evening

Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Group visit to the historic Cellular Jail light & sound show followed by group dinner at a restaurant.

2

Day 2 - Havelock Island

Morning

Group Ferry to Havelock

Private group ferry to Havelock Island, group check-in at resort with comfortable rooms.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach

Group visit to Asia's best beach - swimming, beach volleyball, group games, and group photography.

Evening

Sunset & Group Dinner

Beautiful sunset views followed by a group dinner at the resort with fun group activities.

3

Day 3 - Elephant Beach Adventure

Morning

Elephant Beach Water Sports

Group snorkeling, banana boat rides, jet skiing, glass bottom boat ride, and sea walking at Elephant Beach.

Afternoon

Kalapathar Beach

Group photography session at scenic Kalapathar Beach with unique black rock formations.

Evening

Resort Leisure & Group Games

Fun group games and activities at resort followed by group dinner.

4

Day 4 - Neil Island Day Trip

Morning

Neil Island Excursion

Group ferry to Neil Island, Bharatpur Beach glass bottom boat ride and coral viewing.

Afternoon

Natural Bridge & Laxmanpur Beach

Explore Natural Bridge rock formation and enjoy Laxmanpur Beach with the group.

Evening

Return & Group Dinner

Return to Havelock, group dinner with fun evening activities and group bonding.

5

Day 5 - Departure

Morning

Breakfast & Check-out

Group breakfast, check-out, souvenir shopping, and group photo session before departure.

Afternoon

Ferry & Airport Transfer

Group ferry to Port Blair, AC coach airport transfer, and departure with wonderful group memories.

Package Includes

  • 4 nights group accommodation
  • Daily breakfast for group
  • Airport pickup and drop (AC coach)
  • All ferry transfers
  • Dedicated group tour guide
  • Elephant Beach water sports
  • Glass bottom boat ride
  • Neil Island day trip
  • Group photography session
  • Cellular Jail light & sound show
  • All sightseeing as per itinerary

Not Included

  • Airfare to/from Port Blair
  • Lunch and dinner (except mentioned)
  • Personal expenses and shopping
  • Additional water sports charges
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Any additional services
