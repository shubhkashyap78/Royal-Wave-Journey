Glass Bottom Boats Overview

Discover the underwater world without getting wet! Our glass bottom boats offer a unique window to the vibrant coral reefs and marine life of Andaman. Perfect for families, non-swimmers, and anyone who wants to explore the underwater beauty in comfort.

Key Features

  • Crystal clear glass bottom viewing
  • Comfortable seating with shade
  • Expert marine life guide
  • Safe for all ages and non-swimmers
  • Photography opportunities
  • Educational commentary
  • Refreshments provided
  • Multiple viewing windows

Popular Viewing Spots

30 mins

North Bay Coral Garden

Explore the vibrant coral gardens and tropical fish species.

  • Depth: 3-8 meters
  • Marine Life: Colorful corals, tropical fish
  • Best Time: 9 AM - 4 PM
  • Visibility: Excellent
45 mins

Elephant Beach Reef

Witness the diverse marine ecosystem and coral formations.

  • Depth: 2-6 meters
  • Marine Life: Parrotfish, angelfish, sea turtles
  • Best Time: 10 AM - 3 PM
  • Special: Turtle spotting area
1 hour

Jolly Buoy Island Waters

Premium coral viewing experience in protected marine sanctuary.

  • Depth: 4-10 meters
  • Marine Life: Rare corals, exotic fish
  • Best Time: 11 AM - 2 PM
  • Special: Protected marine sanctuary
