Bharatpur Beach - Neil Island Paradise

Bharatpur Beach is one of the most popular and accessible beaches on Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Known for its shallow waters, vibrant coral reefs, and excellent snorkeling opportunities, this beach offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure for visitors of all ages.

Located just a short walk from the Neil Island jetty, Bharatpur Beach is easily accessible and serves as the gateway to Neil Island's natural beauty. The beach is famous for its calm, shallow waters that extend quite far from the shore, making it ideal for swimming and water activities.

Coral Paradise

Bharatpur Beach is renowned for its living coral reefs that can be observed through glass-bottom boat rides or snorkeling. The crystal-clear waters provide excellent visibility, allowing visitors to witness the vibrant marine ecosystem including colorful fish, sea anemones, and various coral formations. This makes it one of the best spots in the Andaman Islands for underwater exploration and marine life observation.

What to Expect

1

Shallow Water Paradise

Swimming

Safe Swimming Experience

The beach features shallow, calm waters that extend far from the shore, making it perfect for families with children and non-swimmers. The gentle waves and clear water provide a safe and enjoyable swimming experience.

2

Vibrant Coral Reefs

Marine Life

Living Coral Gardens

Explore the stunning coral reefs through snorkeling or glass-bottom boat rides. The healthy coral ecosystem hosts a variety of tropical fish, making it a paradise for marine life enthusiasts and underwater photographers.

3

Glass Bottom Boat Tours

Boat Ride

Underwater Viewing

Experience the underwater world without getting wet through glass-bottom boat tours. These boats provide clear views of the coral reefs and marine life below, perfect for those who prefer to stay dry while exploring.

4

Beach Activities & Relaxation

Leisure

Complete Beach Experience

Enjoy sunbathing on the soft sandy beach, take leisurely walks along the shoreline, or simply relax under the shade of coconut palms. The beach also offers basic amenities and refreshment stalls for visitor convenience.
