    Tour Overview

    Experience the ultimate island-hopping adventure with our comprehensive 4 nights 5 days tour covering Port Blair, Havelock, and Neil Island. This extended journey allows you to explore three distinct islands, each offering unique attractions and experiences in the pristine Andaman archipelago.

    Tour Highlights

    • Visit Fisheries Museum and Naval Marine Museum
    • Corbyn's Cove Beach relaxation
    • Radhanagar Beach - Asia's Best Beach
    • Elephant Beach water sports and snorkeling
    • Neil Island's Bharatpur and Laxmanpur Beaches
    • Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge) exploration
    • Glass bottom boat ride experience

    Detailed Itinerary

    Day 1

    Arrival in Port Blair

    Welcome to the Andaman Islands! Upon arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport, our representative will greet you and assist with transfer to your hotel. After check-in and refreshment, visit the fascinating Fisheries Museum and Naval Marine Museum to learn about marine life and naval history. Spend a relaxing evening at Corbyn's Cove Beach, enjoying the coconut palm-fringed shoreline and gentle waves. Return to hotel for overnight stay.

    Day 2

    Port Blair to Havelock Island

    After breakfast, check-out and proceed to Phoenix Bay Jetty for your ferry journey to Havelock Island. Upon arrival at this tropical paradise, check-in at your beachfront resort and enjoy lunch. Spend the afternoon at the world-renowned Radhanagar Beach, consistently rated among Asia's best beaches. Experience the pristine white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and spectacular sunset views. Enjoy beach activities and swimming before returning to your resort for overnight stay.

    Day 3

    Havelock to Neil Island

    Start with breakfast at your resort before embarking on an exciting boat excursion to Elephant Beach, famous for its vibrant coral reefs and marine life. Enjoy snorkeling, sea walking, and various water sports in this underwater paradise. After lunch at Elephant Beach, take the afternoon ferry to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep). Check-in at your Neil Island accommodation and visit the beautiful Bharatpur Beach, known for its coral formations and clear waters. Overnight stay at Neil Island.

    Day 4

    Neil Island Exploration

    Explore the serene beauty of Neil Island starting with Laxmanpur Beach, famous for its stunning sunrise views and unique rock formations. Visit the Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge), a fascinating coral formation created by nature over thousands of years. Experience Sitapur Beach with its pristine shoreline and peaceful ambiance. Enjoy a glass bottom boat ride to observe colorful coral reefs and tropical fish. In the evening, take the ferry back to Port Blair and check-in at your hotel for overnight stay.

    Day 5

    Departure

    On your final day, visit the historic Cellular Jail National Memorial to learn about India's freedom struggle and the sacrifices made by freedom fighters. If time permits, enjoy the Light and Sound Show that brings history to life. Spend time shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar for souvenirs, local handicrafts, pearls, and shell items. After lunch at a local restaurant, transfer to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of your Andaman island adventure.
