Group Package 8 Nights / 9 Days - Complete Andaman Islands Tour

The ultimate group Andaman experience - our 8 Nights / 9 Days package covers all major islands including Port Blair, Havelock, Neil Island, Baratang, Rangat, and Diglipur. This VIP group package offers premium services, all islands covered, and unforgettable experiences for every group member.

With a dedicated group guide, private AC coaches, private ferry bookings, and specially arranged group activities, this is the most comprehensive group tour available in the Andaman Islands.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Arrival & Port Blair

Morning

VIP Group Welcome & Hotel Check-in

VIP group welcome at airport, AC coach transfer, premium hotel check-in, and welcome refreshments.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove & Samudrika Museum

Group beach activities and educational museum visit.

Evening

Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Group visit followed by welcome group dinner at a premium restaurant.

2

Day 2 - Ross Island & North Bay

Morning

Ross Island Heritage Tour

Group boat trip to Ross Island, explore ruins, deer and peacock spotting, group photography.

Afternoon

North Bay Island Water Sports

Group snorkeling, glass bottom boat ride, sea walking, and underwater photography.

Evening

Local Market & Group Dinner

Souvenir shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar and group dinner.

3

Day 3 - Baratang Island

Morning

Baratang Mangrove Creek Tour

Early morning group drive through Jarawa Reserve Forest and speedboat ride through mangrove creeks.

Afternoon

Limestone Caves & Mud Volcano

Group exploration of limestone caves and the unique mud volcano at Baratang.

Evening

Return to Port Blair

Return journey and group dinner at hotel.

4

Day 4 - Diglipur

Morning

Diglipur - Saddle Peak

Group drive to Diglipur, visit Saddle Peak National Park - the highest point in Andaman, nature trek through lush forests.

Afternoon

Ross & Smith Islands

Group visit to the twin islands connected by a natural sandbar - a unique geographical wonder.

Evening

Return to Port Blair

Return journey and group dinner at hotel.

5

Day 5 - Havelock Island

Morning

Group Ferry to Havelock

Private group ferry to Havelock Island, premium resort check-in with comfortable group rooms.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach

Group visit to Asia's best beach - swimming, beach games, and group photography.

Evening

Sunset & Group Dinner

Sunset views followed by group dinner at the resort.

6

Day 6 - Elephant Beach & Kalapathar

Morning

Elephant Beach Water Adventures

Group snorkeling, banana boat rides, jet skiing, and sea walking at Elephant Beach.

Afternoon

Kalapathar Beach Photography

Group photography session at scenic Kalapathar Beach.

Evening

Resort Leisure & Group Games

Fun group games and activities at resort followed by group dinner.

7

Day 7 - Neil Island

Morning

Neil Island Day Trip

Group ferry to Neil Island, Bharatpur Beach glass bottom boat ride and coral viewing.

Afternoon

Natural Bridge & Laxmanpur Beach

Explore Natural Bridge and enjoy Laxmanpur Beach with the group.

Evening

Return & Group Dinner

Return to Havelock, group dinner at resort.

8

Day 8 - Rangat & Leisure

Morning

Rangat Excursion

Group visit to Amkunj Beach and sea turtle nesting sites at Rangat.

Afternoon

Resort Leisure & Group Games

Relaxing afternoon with fun group activities and bonding time.

Evening

Grand Farewell Group Dinner

Special grand farewell group dinner with fun activities and group photo presentation.

9

Day 9 - Departure

Morning

Breakfast & Check-out

Group breakfast, check-out, souvenir shopping, and final group photo session.

Afternoon

Ferry & Airport Transfer

Group ferry to Port Blair, AC coach airport transfer, and departure with wonderful group memories.
