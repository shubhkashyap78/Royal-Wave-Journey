Romantic Honeymoon Package 3 Nights / 4 Days

Begin your journey of love with our specially curated 3 nights 4 days honeymoon package in the pristine Andaman Islands. This romantic getaway is designed to create unforgettable memories for newlyweds, featuring intimate experiences, luxury accommodations, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Experience the perfect blend of romance and adventure as you explore the crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and lush tropical landscapes of Port Blair and Havelock Island. Our honeymoon package includes romantic candlelight dinners, private boat transfers, and luxury accommodation to make your special trip truly memorable.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Arrival in Port Blair

Morning

Airport Pickup & Hotel Check-in

Arrival at Port Blair Airport, warm welcome with flower garlands, transfer to luxury hotel, check-in and welcome drinks for the couple.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove Beach

Visit the beautiful Corbyn's Cove Beach for a romantic walk along the shoreline and enjoy water sports activities together.

Evening

Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Experience the historical light and sound show at Cellular Jail, followed by a romantic dinner at a seaside restaurant.

2

Day 2 - Havelock Island

Morning

Ferry to Havelock Island

Private ferry transfer to Havelock Island, check-in at beach resort, and enjoy welcome refreshments with ocean views.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach

Visit Asia's best beach - Radhanagar Beach, enjoy romantic moments on pristine white sand and crystal-clear waters.

Evening

Romantic Candlelight Dinner

Special candlelight dinner on the beach with live music, decorated table setup, and personalized service.

3

Day 3 - Adventure & Romance

Morning

Elephant Beach Water Sports

Couple snorkeling at Elephant Beach, explore vibrant coral reefs together, and enjoy other water activities.

Afternoon

Kalapathar Beach

Visit the serene Kalapathar Beach, perfect for romantic photography with unique black rocks and pristine surroundings.

Evening

Sunset Viewing & Spa

Romantic sunset viewing followed by couple spa session at the resort for ultimate relaxation.

4

Day 4 - Departure

Morning

Leisure Time & Check-out

Enjoy leisure time at the resort, final romantic breakfast, check-out and transfer to Port Blair.

Afternoon

Airport Transfer

Transfer to Port Blair Airport for departure with beautiful memories and complimentary photo album.

Package Includes

  • 3 nights luxury accommodation
  • Daily breakfast for couple
  • Airport pickup and drop
  • Private ferry transfers
  • Romantic candlelight dinner
  • Couple spa session
  • All sightseeing as per itinerary
  • Professional photography
  • Welcome drinks and flower decoration
  • Complimentary photo album

Not Included

  • Airfare to/from Port Blair
  • Lunch and dinner (except mentioned)
  • Personal expenses and shopping

  • Any additional services
