Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair

  • Arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport
  • Meet and greet by our representative
  • Transfer to luxury hotel with welcome drinks
  • Check-in and room decoration with flowers and chocolates
  • Evening visit to Corbyn's Cove Beach for sunset
  • Romantic beachside dinner with live music
  • Overnight stay in Port Blair

Day 2: Port Blair Sightseeing

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit to historic Cellular Jail National Memorial
  • Light and Sound Show at Cellular Jail (evening)
  • Explore Chatham Saw Mill and Forest Museum
  • Visit Anthropological Museum
  • Shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar for souvenirs
  • Professional couple photoshoot at scenic locations
  • Candlelight dinner at hotel rooftop
  • Overnight stay in Port Blair

Day 3: Port Blair to Havelock Island

  • Early breakfast and check-out
  • Transfer to Phoenix Bay Jetty
  • Ferry journey to Havelock Island (2.5 hours)
  • Check-in at beach resort
  • Welcome coconut water and tropical fruits
  • Lunch at resort
  • Visit to world-famous Radhanagar Beach
  • Romantic beach walk and sunset viewing
  • Beach photography session
  • Seafood dinner by the beach
  • Overnight stay in Havelock Island

Day 4: Havelock Adventure & Romance

  • Breakfast at resort
  • Elephant Beach excursion with water sports
  • Snorkeling and coral viewing (optional)
  • Sea walking experience for couples
  • Lunch at beachside restaurant
  • Couple spa session with Ayurvedic treatments
  • Visit to KalaPathar Beach for black rocks photography
  • Private beach picnic setup
  • Romantic dinner under the stars
  • Overnight stay in Havelock Island

Day 5: Neil Island Day Trip

  • Early breakfast and check-out
  • Ferry to Neil Island (1 hour)
  • Visit to Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge)
  • Explore Bharatpur Beach with glass bottom boat ride
  • Lunch at local restaurant
  • Visit to Laxmanpur Beach for sunset
  • Coral viewing and beach activities
  • Return ferry to Port Blair
  • Check-in at Port Blair hotel
  • Farewell dinner with cultural program
  • Overnight stay in Port Blair

Day 6: Departure

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Last-minute shopping at Sagarika Emporium
  • Visit to Samudrika Naval Marine Museum (if time permits)
  • Check-out and transfer to airport
  • Departure with beautiful memories
  • Complimentary photo album and souvenir gift
