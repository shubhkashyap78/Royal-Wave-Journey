Kayaking in Andaman Islands

Kayaking in the Andaman Islands is a serene and immersive way to explore the stunning coastlines, hidden coves, and mangrove forests of this tropical paradise. Paddle through the crystal-clear turquoise waters at your own pace and discover the natural beauty of the Andaman Islands up close.

Whether you prefer a leisurely paddle along the beach or an adventurous journey through mangrove creeks, kayaking in Andaman offers something for everyone. The calm, clear waters make it ideal for beginners while the diverse landscapes keep experienced kayakers engaged.

Top kayaking spots in Andaman include Havelock Island, Neil Island, Baratang Mangrove Creek, and Chidiya Tapu. Each location offers a unique kayaking experience with stunning natural surroundings and diverse marine and wildlife encounters.

Kayaking Highlights

Mangrove Kayaking

Adventure

Explore Mangrove Creeks

Paddle through the dense mangrove forests of Baratang and Havelock Island. Spot exotic birds, crabs, and other wildlife as you navigate through the narrow waterways surrounded by lush greenery.

Beach Kayaking

Leisure

Coastal Exploration

Paddle along the pristine beaches of Havelock and Neil Island, exploring hidden coves and secluded beaches that are only accessible by kayak. Enjoy the stunning views of the coastline from the water.

Sunrise & Sunset Kayaking

Magical

Golden Hour on the Water

Experience the magic of kayaking during sunrise or sunset when the sky turns golden and the calm waters reflect the beautiful colors. A truly unforgettable experience in the Andaman Islands.

Safety & Equipment

Safety

Fully Equipped & Guided

All kayaking sessions include life jackets, paddles, and safety equipment. Our experienced guides provide basic training for beginners and accompany all groups for a safe and enjoyable experience.
