Inter-Island Ferries Overview

Travel comfortably between the beautiful islands of Andaman with our reliable ferry services. Our ferries connect all major islands with daily scheduled services, offering comfortable seating and onboard facilities for a pleasant journey.

Key Features

  • Daily scheduled ferry services
  • Comfortable seating arrangements
  • Onboard cafeteria and restrooms
  • Air-conditioned passenger areas
  • Luggage storage facilities
  • Safety equipment and trained crew
  • Wheelchair accessible
  • Entertainment system onboard

Ferry Schedule & Routes

Daily

Port Blair ↔ Havelock Island

Regular ferry service connecting the capital to the most popular tourist destination.

  • Duration: 1.5 - 2 hours
  • Frequency: 4 times daily
  • First Ferry: 6:30 AM
  • Last Ferry: 2:00 PM
Daily

Port Blair ↔ Neil Island

Direct connection to the serene Neil Island with comfortable journey.

  • Duration: 1 - 1.5 hours
  • Frequency: 3 times daily
  • First Ferry: 7:00 AM
  • Last Ferry: 1:30 PM
Daily

Havelock ↔ Neil Island

Inter-island connection for easy island hopping experience.

  • Duration: 45 minutes
  • Frequency: 2 times daily
  • Morning: 10:30 AM
  • Afternoon: 3:30 PM
