Tour Overview

Discover the complete beauty of Andaman with our comprehensive 4 nights 5 days tour covering Port Blair, Havelock, and Neil Island. This well-balanced package offers the perfect combination of history, adventure, relaxation, and natural beauty, making it ideal for families, groups, and travelers seeking a complete Andaman experience.

Tour Highlights

  • Samudrika Naval Marine Museum exploration
  • Corbyn's Cove Beach relaxation
  • Radhanagar Beach - World's best beach
  • Elephant Beach water sports adventure
  • Neil Island's pristine Bharatpur Beach
  • Laxmanpur Beach sunrise experience
  • Natural Bridge geological wonder

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Port Blair Exploration

Begin your Andaman journey with arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport where our tour coordinator will meet and assist you. After comfortable transfer to hotel and check-in, enjoy lunch at the hotel restaurant. Visit the informative Samudrika Naval Marine Museum to learn about marine life and naval heritage. Spend a relaxing evening at Corbyn's Cove Beach, enjoying the coconut palm-fringed shoreline and gentle waves. Return to hotel for dinner and overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 2

Port Blair to Havelock Island

After breakfast, check-out and transfer to Phoenix Bay Jetty for your ferry journey to Havelock Island. Upon arrival at this tropical paradise, check-in at your beach resort and enjoy lunch with sea views. Spend the afternoon at the world-renowned Radhanagar Beach, consistently rated among the world's best beaches. Experience pristine white sand, crystal-clear waters, and spectacular sunset views. Enjoy beach activities, swimming, and photography before returning to your resort for dinner and overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 3

Havelock to Neil Island

Start with breakfast at your resort before embarking on an exciting boat excursion to Elephant Beach, famous for its vibrant coral reefs and marine biodiversity. Enjoy snorkeling, sea walking, and various water sports activities in this underwater paradise. After lunch at Elephant Beach, take the afternoon ferry to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep). Check-in at your Neil Island accommodation and visit the beautiful Bharatpur Beach, known for its excellent coral formations and clear waters perfect for swimming. Overnight stay at Neil Island.

Day 4

Neil Island Complete Tour

Experience the magical sunrise at Sitapur Beach, one of the most beautiful sunrise points in Andaman. After breakfast at your accommodation, visit Laxmanpur Beach with its stunning rock formations and peaceful ambiance. Explore the Natural Bridge (Howrah Bridge), a fascinating coral formation created by nature over thousands of years. Enjoy a glass bottom boat ride to observe colorful coral reefs and tropical fish. In the evening, take the ferry back to Port Blair and check-in at your hotel for overnight stay.

Day 5

Cellular Jail & Departure

After breakfast and check-out, visit the historic Cellular Jail National Memorial to learn about India's freedom struggle and the sacrifices made by freedom fighters. If time permits, enjoy the Light and Sound Show that brings history to life through dramatic narration. Spend time shopping at Aberdeen Bazaar for souvenirs, local handicrafts, pearls, and shell items. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before transferring to the airport for your departure, carrying beautiful memories of your comprehensive Andaman island adventure.
