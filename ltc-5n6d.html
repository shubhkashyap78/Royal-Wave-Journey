LTC Package 5 Nights / 6 Days - Port Blair, Havelock, Neil & Ross Island

Make the most of your LTC benefits with our 5 Nights / 6 Days Andaman package covering Port Blair, Havelock Island, Neil Island, and the historic Ross Island. All stays are at government-approved hotels with complete LTC documentation and GST bills provided.

Our dedicated LTC team ensures all paperwork is in order for hassle-free reimbursement. Enjoy a comprehensive Andaman experience while we take care of all the documentation requirements.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1 - Arrival & Port Blair

Morning

Airport Welcome & Hotel Check-in

Welcome at airport, transfer to LTC-approved hotel, check-in with proper GST bill.

Afternoon

Corbyn's Cove & Samudrika Museum

Visit Corbyn's Cove Beach and Samudrika Marine Museum with all entry receipts.

Evening

Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show

Visit Cellular Jail light & sound show followed by dinner with proper bill.

2

Day 2 - Ross Island & North Bay

Morning

Ross Island Heritage Tour

Boat trip to Ross Island, explore British-era ruins, deer and peacock spotting with boat tickets provided.

Afternoon

North Bay Island Water Sports

Snorkeling, glass bottom boat ride, and sea walking at North Bay Island with all receipts.

Evening

Local Market & Dinner

Evening at Aberdeen Bazaar and dinner at approved restaurant with proper bill.

3

Day 3 - Havelock Island

Morning

Ferry to Havelock Island

Ferry transfer with proper tickets, check-in at LTC-approved resort with GST bill.

Afternoon

Radhanagar Beach

Visit Asia's best beach for swimming, sightseeing, and photography.

Evening

Sunset & Dinner

Sunset views followed by dinner at resort with proper GST bill.

4

Day 4 - Elephant Beach & Kalapathar

Morning

Elephant Beach Water Activities

Snorkeling, banana boat rides, and glass bottom boat ride at Elephant Beach with all receipts.

Afternoon

Kalapathar Beach

Visit scenic Kalapathar Beach with unique black rock formations.

Evening

Resort Leisure & Dinner

Relaxing evening at resort followed by dinner with proper bill.

5

Day 5 - Neil Island

Morning

Neil Island Day Trip

Ferry to Neil Island with proper tickets, Bharatpur Beach glass bottom boat ride.

Afternoon

Natural Bridge & Laxmanpur Beach

Explore Natural Bridge and enjoy Laxmanpur Beach.

Evening

Return & Dinner

Return to Havelock, dinner at resort with proper bill.

6

Day 6 - Departure

Morning

Breakfast & Check-out

Final breakfast, hotel check-out with complete GST bill, and souvenir shopping.

Afternoon

Ferry & Airport Transfer

Ferry to Port Blair, airport transfer, and departure with all LTC bills and documentation handed over.

  • 5 nights LTC-approved accommodation
